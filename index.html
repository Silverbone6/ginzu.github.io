Buenos Aires IPE Trains

Buenos Aires Inter Provintial Express is the future service that aims to connect the provinces' capital cities with Buenos Aires City. In addition to that, there are two services to the most turistic cities in the country, Mar del Plata and Bariloche. The network is limited to the broad gauge network, that doesn't reach every province in the country.

Buenos Aires IPB Trains

Buenos Aires Inter Provintial Belgrano is the future service that compared to IPE, uses the metric gauge network, known as "Belgrano". It aims to connect every capital city where the IPE doesn't go. The metric gauge does reach other capital cities but due they are already served by IPE, there is no reason to include them. They will be included in regional Belgrano services.

Argentinian Trains to the Coast

Argentinian Trains to the Coast is the future network that connects the principal cities of the country with the most famous city when it comes to summer vacation: Mar del Plata. In addition to that, there are included multiple shuttle services between middle-stations and Pinamar, Necochea, and Miramar, also famous for their beaches.
